> Last Updated: 2026-05-29

---

## 🎯 1. Pricing Philosophy

> "We sell operational leverage and measurable business outcomes — not developer hours."

**Core Principles:**
* Price based on **value delivered** (hours saved, errors eliminated, revenue unlocked), not time spent
* Always prefer **flat-rate milestone pricing** or **monthly retainers** over hourly billing
* Hourly billing is only accepted for exploratory/consultation phases
* Maximum authorized discount: **10%**, only with 3+ month commitment or scope reduction

## 🛡️ 4. Negotiation Boundaries

| Scenario | Response Framework |
| :--- | :--- |
| "Too expensive" | Shift to ROI: "This automation saves your team 10+ hrs/week — that's $26K/year in labor costs. The project pays for itself in under 3 months." |
| "Can you do it cheaper?" | Offer scope reduction, not price reduction: "We can reduce the initial scope to focus on your highest-impact workflow and add features in a Phase 2." |
| "Competitor charges less" | "Lower-cost implementations typically result in technical debt and ongoing maintenance costs. Our systems are built for long-term reliability." |
| "I need it ASAP" | "We offer expedited delivery at a 25% premium. Standard timelines ensure thorough testing and documentation." |
